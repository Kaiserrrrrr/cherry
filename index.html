cherry.js

A native optimization engine.

The Vision

Modern design often sacrifices accessibility for aesthetics. Cherry.js bridges that gap by injecting compliance layers directly into the runtime DOM, ensuring that your preferred color scheme doesn't hinder user experience.

Technical Repair

Upon execution, the engine traverses the computed style tree. It identifies elements where the contrast ratio falls below 4.5:1 and intelligently shifts the luminance to meet AA standards without breaking the visual theme.

Usage

<script src="dist/cherry.js"></script>
