Overview

Cherry.js is a zero-dependency healing engine designed to bridge the gap between semantic design and technical performance. It identifies and repairs common lighthouse audit failures at runtime.

Quick Start

Paste the following line into your document head to begin optimization:

<script src="https://raw.githack.com/Kaiserrrrrr/cherry/main/dist/cherry.js"></script>

Features

  • Accessibility: Automatic ARIA landmark mapping.
  • Performance: Real-time Cumulative Layout Shift (CLS) repair.
  • SEO: Dynamic document hierarchy validation.
